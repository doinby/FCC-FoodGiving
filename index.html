Our Mission

Food Giving is created in response to the pressing need to consume food responsibly. 4 billion tons of edible food is wasted each year, but it won't stay that way if each person take a small step toward preventing food waste. To assist with this process, our platform is offering an easy gateway between the donator and the receiver. And the best part, it's completely FREE!
